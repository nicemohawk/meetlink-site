MeetLink

Google Meet & Zoom, native in Apple Calendar.

MeetLink adds Google Meet and Zoom as video call options in Calendar.app's "Add Video Call" dropdown. Sign in once, and every new event is one click away from a meeting link.

Coming soon on the Mac App Store

How it works

  1. Sign in with your Google or Zoom account in the MeetLink app
  2. Open Calendar.app and create or edit an event
  3. Click "Add Video Call" and choose Google Meet or Zoom
  4. A fresh meeting link is created and attached to the event

Your data stays on your Mac

Credentials are stored in the macOS Keychain. MeetLink talks directly to Google and Zoom — no intermediary servers, no analytics, no tracking.
