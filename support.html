Common issues

MeetLink doesn't appear in Calendar's "Add Video Call" menu

After installing MeetLink, you may need to restart Calendar.app for the extension to appear. If it still doesn't show, open System Settings > General > Login Items & Extensions > Calendar and make sure MeetLink is enabled.

Sign-in fails or tokens expire

Try signing out in the MeetLink app and signing back in. If the issue persists, revoke MeetLink's access from your Google or Zoom account settings, then re-authorize.

How do I remove my data?

Sign out in the MeetLink app to immediately delete all stored credentials. See our Privacy Policy for full details on data retention and deletion.

App removal

To stop using MeetLink:

  1. Open the MeetLink app and sign out of all providers
  2. Revoke access from your Google and/or Zoom account settings
  3. Delete MeetLink from your Applications folder

Signing out deletes all stored credentials from your Mac's Keychain. No data is retained on any external server.
