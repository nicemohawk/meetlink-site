MeetLink

Zoom Integration

Note: MeetLink is not yet publicly available. This documentation is for an upcoming feature currently under review.

Overview

MeetLink adds Zoom as a video call option in Apple Calendar on macOS. Once you sign in with your Zoom account, you can create Zoom meetings directly from Calendar.app's "Add Video Call" menu — no need to open Zoom or copy-paste links.

Prerequisites

  • macOS 15.0 (Sequoia) or later
  • A Zoom account (free or paid)
  • MeetLink installed from the Mac App Store

Getting started

1. Install MeetLink

Download MeetLink from the Mac App Store and open the app.

2. Sign in with Zoom

Click "Sign in with Zoom" in the MeetLink app. You'll be redirected to Zoom's authorization page in your browser. Sign in with your Zoom credentials and grant MeetLink permission to create meetings on your behalf.

3. Enable the Calendar extension

If prompted, enable MeetLink's Calendar extension in System Settings > General > Login Items & Extensions > Calendar. This allows MeetLink to appear in Calendar.app's video call menu.

4. Create a Zoom meeting

  1. Open Calendar.app and create or edit an event
  2. Click "Add Video Call"
  3. Select "Zoom" from the dropdown
  4. A new Zoom meeting link is automatically created and attached to the event

What MeetLink accesses

MeetLink requests two Zoom OAuth scopes:

  • meeting:write — to create new Zoom meetings
  • user:read — to display your email address in the app

MeetLink does not access your existing meetings, recordings, chat messages, or any other Zoom data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Managing your account

Sign out

Open the MeetLink app and click "Sign Out" next to your Zoom account. This immediately deletes all stored Zoom credentials from your Mac.

Revoke access

To revoke MeetLink's access to your Zoom account, visit Zoom's installed apps page and remove MeetLink.

Removing MeetLink

  1. Open the MeetLink app and sign out of Zoom
  2. Revoke access from your Zoom account settings
  3. Delete MeetLink from your Applications folder

Signing out deletes all stored credentials from your Mac's Keychain. MeetLink has no backend server, so no data is retained anywhere after removal.

Troubleshooting

Zoom doesn't appear in the "Add Video Call" menu

Restart Calendar.app after installing MeetLink. If Zoom still doesn't appear, check that MeetLink's Calendar extension is enabled in System Settings > General > Login Items & Extensions > Calendar.

Sign-in fails or meeting creation errors

Sign out of Zoom in the MeetLink app and sign back in. If the problem persists, revoke MeetLink's access from your Zoom account settings and re-authorize.
